ABOUT

Hey there! My name is Adrian. I'm a former civil engineer turned full-stack web developer. I enjoy building things and bringing concepts to life. Over the past 5 years, I worked comfortably as a civil engineer - Creating aesthetically pleasing facades for buildings that were occupied by companies such as Google, Linkedin, and Stanford. Despite having a steady career and being able marvel at a completed structure I help build, I wasn't passionate about the work I did and grew bored.

I was always fascinated by tech growing up and have always been around computers. My family always turned towards me for I.T. related issues and I was always tinkering with software. I decided to take a few online programming courses to gauge my interest in programming. A few hours later, I knew that this was the career for me. I enjoyed the problem solving aspects of it as well as the struggle/challenge of getting code to run. I still throw my hands up in the air and scream "Yes!" everytime I figure something out. So I enrolled in Thinkful/Bloc's full-stack web development track to accelerate my learning in hopes of switching careers. Check out some of the cool stuff I've built below!
